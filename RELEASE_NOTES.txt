Here are the features in this release:
-an appointment page with a search and filter feature for users to look up their doctor and see if he/she is running late with doctor biographies and pictures
-a bird's eye view map of the Cancer Center and the surrounding buildings and roads in 3D and a 2D map plan
-a list of all the resources (educational classes, support groups, points of interest) with their description and pictures
- a today's event page with clickable links that connects to the calendar of events
- a contact page with 3 numbers important to the cancer center with click-to-call links


Currently compatible with iOS 9.0 and higher
